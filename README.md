### Background Story

In the afternoon around 16:00 until late at night when the weather is favorable Maccareta is a young man around 35 years old wants to refresh the mind, refresh and nourish the body by exercising both jogging and running in green areas without worrying about the dangers of motorized vehicles.
By running and jogging with clear directions and goals in the GOP area does help Macc get new records, as well as refreshing. But monotonous routes and the same tracks that are repeated over and over again make the mileage less long and get bored quickly.

### Our Solutions

_uses Core Location and MapKit to help SOBARI (Sohabat Lari) to choose their own track based their own positions, conditions and needs, so that SOLARI can get route, waypoint, and information based on their needs_
